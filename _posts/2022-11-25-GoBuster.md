GoBuster 
Description: 
    - -u The target URL 
    - -w Path to your wordlist 
    - -U and -P Username and Password for Basic Auth 
    - -c <http cookies> Specify a cookie for simulating your auth

`gobuster dir-u http://<ip>:<port> -w <word_list_location>`
